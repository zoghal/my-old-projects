Here is IRAN-HAMADAN

Hamadan province with 20, 172 kilometers measurement is one

of the western provinces in IRAN. The capital of this province

is Hamadan city with the historical antecedent from 7 century BC.

Laljin district, one of the Hamadan district with the extensive

equal to 508 square kilometers at the 48 degrees longitude and 29

minutes eastern, 24 degrees latitude and 50 minutes northern and

with 990 meters of height from the sea level has been located in 21

kilometers of the northwestern of Hamadan.

There are numerous mines of high quality of clay in Laljin zone

and because of that the production of earthenware (baked clay)

have many flourish and 80 percent of the residents are engaged on

the production and selling of earthenware in Laljin zone of Hamadan.

There are 848 workshops in Laljin that approximately 45000

persons work in these workshops. Each workshop consumes 70

tons of clay in a year on the average and about 6000 tons of clay

is consumed in these workshops yearly.

All of the earthenware productions of this region are made in traditional

method and are entirely by hands by the craftsmen and the workmen.
